## Repository Overview

This repository contains documentation for CQBCC (长清宝创诚) beauty/cosmetic medical service business. The primary content consists of Chinese-language PDF files documenting service offerings, price lists, and treatment categories.

## Business Domain

**Medical Aesthetics/Cosmetic Treatments**

The service catalog includes:
- Light-based treatments (IPL, laser, picosecond)
- Radiofrequency treatments (RF microneedling, HIFU)
- Injectable treatments (hyaluronic acid fillers, collagen, botulinum toxin, regenerative materials)
- Chemical peels (salicylic acid, trichloroacetic acid)
- Skin management and maintenance programs

## Treatment Categories

### Major Treatment Areas:
1. **Skin Beauty** (皮肤美容): Hydration, brightening, oil control, pore minimization
2. **Problem Skin** (问题肌肤治疗): Redness, pigmentation, acne
3. **Anti-aging** (抗衰年轻化): Eye area, perioral area, neck, facial contouring
4. **Device Treatments** (仪器治疗): Various laser and RF technologies
5. **Injectables** (注射填充): Fillers and wrinkle reduction
6. **Body Treatments**: Hair removal, body contouring
